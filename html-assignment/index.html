About Me

Hi everyone, my name is Alexander Urbach. I'm 18 years old and my birthday is on the 27th of September (one week from now). I have a few hobbies like skateboarding, snowboarding and sewing/fashion design. When I want to achieve something in any of my hobbies (such as learning a trick or creating a new garment) I tend to put a lot of time and energy into the process to achieve it as perfect as possible. Another thing I enjoy doing is casual reading. One of my favorite books is titled "The Alchemist".

Here's a good quote from the book:

And, when you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it.
-Paulo Coelho, The Alchemist

Another thing I'm passionate about would be creating music. I've been playing guitar for around 7 years and producing music on FL Studio for around 3-5 years. I don't focus on any specific genre and I tend to spend hours in my room experimenting with different sounds. When it comes to my style, I tend to prefer more airy and ethereal sounding melodies.

I like web design and I've done work and made sales on Shopify in the E-Commerce field for a few months. I think this class is one of my favourites due to the fact that it involves a little less abstract work than the others but I still have the opportunity to be creative when writing code.
